About Us



Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the most accurate and up-to-date traffic information to help you avoid traffic jams, get to your destination on time, and make the most of your time on the road.

Our History

Sganyfc.com was founded in 2008 by a group of friends who were frustrated with the lack of accurate and up-to-date traffic information available. We set out to change that, and today we are the leading provider of traffic information in the United States.

Our team

Our team of traffic experts are constantly monitoring the roads and updating our traffic information in real-time. This way, you can be sure that you always have the most accurate and up-to-date information to help you avoid traffic jams and get to your destination on time.

Our commitment to you

We are committed to providing you with the best traffic information available, and we will continue to work hard to make sure that you have the information you need to make the most of your time on the road.
